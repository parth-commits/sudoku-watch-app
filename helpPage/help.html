This is the classic game of
Sudoku. Fill the board such that each row, column and inner box has unique numbers 1-9. Tapping       on a small box will enlarge it. There you may tap on any of its
9 squares to cycle through
the numbers 1-9.
Tap outside the box area to
make it small again. Press to
get a random hint. Don't cheat yourself and use it only when absolutely necessary. Press to clear all input. Tired of the
current board? Press to
get a new board.
